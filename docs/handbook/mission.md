# Mission & vision (from public sources)

## Mission (expressed on the official site)
Ten Lives “helps regenerate land” and aims to show how people can “repair life, nature, and ourselves” when coming together to do meaningful work and celebrate. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

## Vision (expressed on LinkedIn)
Ten Lives frames its work as a response to desertification risk in Mediterranean Europe and eco-anxiety, using regenerative festivals as a “do and dance community.” :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
